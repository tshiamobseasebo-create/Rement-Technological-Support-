REMNANT TECH SUPPORT

 

Call Escalation Flowshart

 

If issue unresolved at one level, escalate to the next level.

 

1. Frontline Support Basic Troubleshooting

(password reset, connectivity, FAQs)

       Password reset If you forgot your password and cannot log in, this team helps you create a new one.

       Connectivity If your computer or phone cannot connect to the internet, Wi-Fi, or network, they check cables, settings, or signals to fix it.

       FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions) These are common questions that many people ask. For example: How do I print? or How do I open this program?. The frontline team answers these quickly.

2. Second Level Support Complex Troubleshooting

(Software errors, configuration)

       Software errors Sometimes a program or application stops working, freezes, or gives an error message. This team knows how to check deeper and fix these problems.

       Configuration This means setting up systems or programs, so they work properly. Example: changing settings in your email, adjusting software so it runs on your computer, or setting up printers.

3. Third Level Support Critical Issues

(Server failures, vendor liaison)

       Server failures A server is like the big computer that many people use at the same time. If it crashes or stops working, many workers cant do their jobs. This team handles those very serious breakdowns.

       Vendor liaison Sometimes the company needs help from outside specialists (the people who made the software, hardware, or service). The third-level team talks to those vendors (suppliers) to get the problem solved.
